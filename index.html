What is HUNTER TOKEN?

HUNTER TOKEN (HTR) brings about efficiency and decentralisation to sure community.

Developed off the need for accountability and speed, it will feature – with security – sure tokens as a major asset and other cryptocurrencies to trade with.

HTR TOKEN is powered by the community and distributed to its highly motivated members, starting and unlocking new milestones in the project will be determined by cumulative efforts through the process of voting.Votes will be locked up in form of staking and released with maturity at the completion within the given timeframe as well as gaining its value thru multiple exchange platforms as part of its milestones.
